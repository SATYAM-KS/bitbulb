FAQ

Asked Questions

We know every project is unique, and you might
have some questions before getting started.

1
What services do you offer?

We specialize in three core areas:

  • Software Development: Web apps, mobile apps, AI bots & agents, and enterprise systems
  • Web Design: UI/UX design, responsive design, interaction design, and dashboards
  • Marketing: SEO, content creation, social media, campaigns, and analytics

We provide complete digital solutions to help your business grow.

2
How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary by project scope:

  • Websites: 4-6 weeks
  • Custom Software: 8-16 weeks
  • Smaller Projects: 2-3 weeks

We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

3
Do you offer support after project completion?

Yes. We offer ongoing support and maintenance packages including:

  • Technical updates and bug fixes
  • Security patches
  • Performance monitoring
  • Content updates

Our team ensures your digital solutions continue to perform optimally as your business grows.

4
Can you work with startups and small businesses?

Absolutely. We work with businesses of all sizes and offer:

  • Flexible, scalable solutions
  • Budget-friendly packages
  • Tailored strategies for growth

We're committed to helping startups and small businesses build a strong digital foundation and scale successfully.

5
How do you price your services?

Pricing is customized based on your project needs:

  • Fixed packages for standard services
  • Custom quotes for larger projects
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden costs

We'll discuss your budget during our consultation to find the right solution for you.
